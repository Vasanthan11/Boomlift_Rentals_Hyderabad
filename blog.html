Help & FAQs

Common Questions from Hyderabad Clients

Over time we noticed the same doubts appearing across projects. Here are a few of them, along with straightforward answers from our rental team.

  • Do you visit the site before confirming a rental?

    For straightforward jobs we can often confirm based on drawings and photos. For complex access or tight deadlines, a short site visit or video call helps us suggest a safer option.

  • Can one machine be shared between multiple locations in the city?

    Yes, provided loading, permits and travel time are considered upfront. We frequently plan multi-location schedules across Hyderabad and nearby industrial areas.

  • What if weather or approvals delay my work?

    Let us know as soon as you foresee a delay. In many cases we can reschedule or adjust reporting times, especially for long-duration rentals.

  • Can you support shutdowns running 24/7?

    Yes, for planned shutdowns we can arrange round-the-clock support and additional backup machines when needed, subject to prior confirmation and availability.
