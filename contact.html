Help & FAQs

Common questions from Hyderabad clients

We see similar doubts across projects. Here are a few quick answers so you can plan confidently.

Do you visit the site before confirming a rental?

For complex jobs we may visit or review site photos/video. For straightforward works, details shared over call or WhatsApp are usually enough.

Can one machine be shared between multiple locations?

Yes, as long as travel time and access conditions are workable. We’ll factor this into the quote and schedule.

What happens if rain or approvals delay the work?

Inform us at the earliest. In many cases we can reschedule or adjust reporting based on machine availability and transport commitments.

Need a second opinion on your access plan?

If you’re unsure whether scaffolding, boom lifts or scissor lifts are best for your job, send us your layout and scope of work. We’ll share practical suggestions based on similar projects we’ve handled.

You’ll get clear notes on options, pros/cons and typical cost differences so your team can take a call.
