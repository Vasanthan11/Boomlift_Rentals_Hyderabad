Scissor lift FAQs

  • Can scissor lifts be used on tiled or epoxy floors?

    Yes, our indoor units have non-marking tyres and are suited for level, finished floors. We will confirm the floor load requirement with you.

  • Do you provide lifts with platform extensions?

    Many of our models have slide-out deck extensions that give extra outreach for racking, ducting and conveyor work.

  • What power is required for charging electric scissors?

    Standard industrial 230V / 16A supply is usually sufficient. Our team will share exact requirements for the selected model.

  • Can the same lift be moved between multiple buildings?

    Yes, subject to access and loading constraints. We can plan inter-building movements or recommend separate units if that is safer.
